Our Certificaton Process

To maintain a high standard of practice, NYPSCB has established a robust certification process. This process mandates that Peer Specialists not only acquire necessary training but also undergo supervised sessions, culminating in a comprehensive examination. This rigorous approach ensures that certified Peer Specialists possess both the knowledge and competence essential for their sensitive role in the community.

Upholding Professional Ethics and Standards

Beyond initial certification, the NYPSCB plays a continuous role in the professional journey of Peer Specialists. We oversee the renewal and recertification procedures, emphasizing the importance of adhering to ethical guidelines. Additionally, we provide a structured venue for grievances, ensuring that the integrity and credibility of our Peer Specialists remain unimpeachable.
